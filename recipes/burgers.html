Juicy Burger Patties

Description

Create the ultimate homemade burger patties with this simple and flavorful recipe. Using high-quality ground beef, seasonings, and a touch of Worcestershire sauce, these patties are shaped to perfection for a juicy, mouthwatering bite. Grilled or pan-seared, they develop a delicious crust while remaining tender and flavorful inside. Customize your patties with your favorite herbs and spices, and serve them on toasted buns with all the classic toppingsâ€”lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and your choice of sauce. Perfect for backyard barbecues or casual weeknight dinners, these burger patties are sure to satisfy any burger lover!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1/2 cup crushed saltine crackers or Pank bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

Note: If you substitute regular dried breadcrumbs for the crackers or panko, reduce to 1/4 cup. They are denser and will create a "meatloaf texture" if you add the regular amount.

  1. Set out a large mixing bowl. Add in the ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper. Mix by hand until the meat mixture is smooth, but stop once the mixture looks even. (Overmixing can create a dense heavy texture)
  2. Press the meat down in the bowl, into an even disk.
  3. Set out a baking sheet, lined with wax paper or foil, to hold the patties. One at a time, gather the patty mix and press firmly into patties. Shape them just slightly larger than the buns you plan to use, to account for shrinkage during cooking. Set the patties on the baking sheet. Use a spoon to press a dent in the center of each patty so they don't puff up as they cook. If you need to stack the patties, separate them with a sheet of wax paper.
  4. Preheat the grill or a skillet to medium heat. (Approximately 350-400 degrees F.)
  5. For thick patties: Grill or fry the patties for 3-4 minutes per side
  6. For thin patties: Cook on the griddle for 2 minutes per side.
