Mango Sorbet

Description

Indulge in the refreshing taste of homemade mango sorbet, a delightful frozen treat perfect for warm days. This simple recipe combines ripe, juicy mangoes with a touch of sugar and a splash of lime juice to enhance the natural sweetness and flavor. Blended until smooth and frozen to perfection, this sorbet is not only a delicious dessert but also a light and fruity option for any occasion. Serve it in chilled bowls or cones for a vibrant and satisfying treat that captures the essence of summer in every bite!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1-2 tbsp simple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add frozen mango, lime juice, coconut milk, and simple syrup (optional) into a food processor. Blend for 20-30 seconds.
  2. Use a spoon to scrape the sides so the mango can continue to mix.
  3. Continue blending until preferred consistency is met.
  4. scoop into chilled bowls and enjoy!
