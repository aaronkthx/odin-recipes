Baked Ziti

Description

This tasty baked ziti dish cooked in a cast iron dutch oven over an open fire will make your friends and family asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ziti pasta (cooked)
  • 1 lb. bulk Italian sausage
  • Green Peppers (diced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • Mozzarella chcese
  • About 24 coals (if cooking over fire)

Instructions

  1. In a Dutch oven sitting on bottom heat, brown sausage until done.
  2. Add peppers and onions, stir and cook until crisp tender, 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook another 2 minutes. (With the veggies, add as much or as little as you like, omit what you don't like.)
  4. Add cooked pasta and spaghetti sauce.
  5. Add cubed or grated cheese and stir well.
  6. Cover and bake for about 30 minutes with 16 coals on top and 8 under, until heated through.
  7. Add more grated cheese on top and cover to melt.
