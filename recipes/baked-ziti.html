Baked Ziti

Description

Savor the comforting flavors of baked ziti, a classic Italian-American dish that brings together al dente pasta, rich marinara sauce, and a blend of melted cheeses. This easy-to-make recipe layers ziti pasta with a hearty meat sauce or roasted vegetables, then tops it all with gooey mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Baked until bubbly and golden, this dish is perfect for family gatherings, potlucks, or a cozy dinner at home. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal thatâ€™s sure to please everyone at the table!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ziti pasta (cooked)
  • 1 lb. bulk Italian sausage
  • Green Peppers (diced)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • Mozzarella chcese
  • About 24 coals (if cooking over fire)

Instructions

  1. In a Dutch oven sitting on bottom heat, brown sausage until done.
  2. Add peppers and onions, stir and cook until crisp tender, 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook another 2 minutes. (With the veggies, add as much or as little as you like, omit what you don't like.)
  4. Add cooked pasta and spaghetti sauce.
  5. Add cubed or grated cheese and stir well.
  6. Cover and bake for about 30 minutes with 16 coals on top and 8 under, until heated through.
  7. Add more grated cheese on top and cover to melt.
